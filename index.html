Process

From first enquiry to
long-term client. Automated.

01

Client enquiry arrives

Via web form, email, WhatsApp, or referral link — Augmentra captures it all in one place from the first moment of contact.

02

AI scores and routes

Every enquiry gets an instant AI quality score. Urgent cases are flagged for immediate attention; routine cases move through automated workflows without manual input.

03

Documents requested automatically

A personalised document checklist is sent to the client. Follow-up reminders are handled by Augmentra — not your team — until everything is received.

04

Case moves through pipeline

AI continuously updates case status, surfaces blockers, and keeps your team informed. Everyone knows exactly where every client stands, in real time.

05

Completed and beyond

Post-completion, Augmentra monitors for retention opportunities. Renewal dates, upsell signals, and re-engagement triggers — nothing gets forgotten after close.
