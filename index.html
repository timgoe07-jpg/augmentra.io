What Augmentra does

Six AI agents. One platform.

Each agent handles a distinct part of the broker workflow — end to end.

🎯

Lead Qualifier

Scores every new enquiry 0–100 based on urgency, income type, loan size, and contact completeness. Triggers the right next action automatically.

Score: 91 → Book call Score: 62 → Send checklist
📝

Deal Digest

Drop a voice note or text update into a deal. The agent extracts borrower type, property type, loan amount, and generates a structured task list.

Document Collector

How it works

From enquiry to post-settlement

Augmentra sits behind your existing workflow and handles everything that doesn't need a human.

01

Lead comes in

Via your website, CRM, or WhatsApp. Augmentra picks it up instantly and runs qualification scoring.

02

AI scores & routes

High-value leads flagged for immediate callback. Warm leads get your checklist automatically. Low scores get nurture flows.

03

Deal is briefed

Broker drops a voice note. Deal Digest agent extracts the key facts, creates tasks, and syncs to your pipeline.

04

Docs auto-chased

Augmentra knows what's needed. Missing docs trigger personalised client emails — no manual drafting.

05

Settlement & beyond

Post-settlement portal tracks the full loan lifecycle. Repricing opportunities surfaced automatically. Nothing falls through the cracks.
